<p><p>The epoxy powder coating market is primarily divided into two types: fast curing and common curing types. Fast curing epoxy powder coatings are designed to harden quickly under heat, allowing for streamlined production processes and reduced cycle times. In contrast, common curing types offer a more traditional curing rate, providing flexibility for various applications but taking longer to set. Both types are valued for their durability and corrosion resistance in diverse industries, including automotive, furniture, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
