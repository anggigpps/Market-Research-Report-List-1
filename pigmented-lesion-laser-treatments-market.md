<p><p>Pigmented lesion laser treatments encompass various laser technologies designed to target and treat skin discolorations. The Q-Switched Nd:YAG Laser employs a neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet crystal, providing deeper penetration for treating various pigmented lesions with minimal damage to surrounding tissue.
