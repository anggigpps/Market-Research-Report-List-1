The technology is increasingly used for rapid prototyping, custom tooling, and even end-use parts, significantly enhancing design flexibility and reducing lead times.</p><p>The 3D printing market in engineering and manufacturing is experiencing substantial growth, driven by advancements in materials, machine capabilities, and software integration. Industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare are at the forefront, leveraging 3D printing for lightweight components, customized implants, and prototyping solutions. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period, reflecting rising investments and increasing adoption across various sectors.
