<p><p>Energy harvesting systems capture ambient energy from various sources to power devices. Photovoltaic systems convert sunlight into electricity using solar panels, widely used in both residential and commercial applications. Thermoelectric systems exploit temperature differences to generate power, often employed in industrial processes. Piezoelectric systems harness mechanical stress from vibrations or movements, suitable for wearable technology. Electrodynamic systems utilize electromagnetic induction, commonly found in energy generation from moving vehicles or machinery, translating motion into usable electrical energy.</p></p>
