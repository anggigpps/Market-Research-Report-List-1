<p><strong>The Light Level Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lux (lx)</li><li>Foot Candle (fc)</li><li>Candela per Square Meter (cd/m²)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light level meter market encompasses various measurement units used to quantify illumination. Lux (lx) measures light intensity per square meter, commonly used in indoor settings. Foot Candle (fc) measures lumens per square foot, often used in architectural applications. Candela per square meter (cd/m²), or nit, gauges luminance, crucial for displays and screens. Each unit serves distinct industries, ensuring optimal lighting for different applications, from photography to office environments, influencing purchasing decisions and product designs.</p></p>
