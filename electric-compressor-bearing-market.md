<p><ul><li>Double Row Angular Contact Ball Bearing</li><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric compressor bearing market features various types, with double row angular contact ball bearings and deep groove ball bearings being key segments. Double row angular contact ball bearings offer high load capacity and rigidity, ideal for applications requiring precise alignment and support under axial and radial loads. Conversely, deep groove ball bearings are versatile, accommodating both axial and radial forces efficiently, making them suitable for general electric compressor operations. Both types enhance performance, reliability, and longevity in electric compressor systems.</p></p>
