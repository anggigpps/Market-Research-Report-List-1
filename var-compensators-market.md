<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Var Compensators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCR-typed SVC</li><li>MCR- typed SVC</li><li>TSC- typed SVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Var Compensators operate in various market types to enhance voltage stability and reactive power management. TCR-typed SVC (Thyristor Controlled Reactor) adjusts reactive power dynamically by controlling the firing angle of thyristors, offering rapid response. MCR-typed SVC (Mechanically Switched Capacitor) employs capacitors switched on and off manually or automatically to manage reactive power intermittently. TSC-typed SVC (Thyristor Switched Capacitor) combines the benefits of rapid thyristor switching with capacitive compensation for better voltage support, enhancing overall grid performance.</p></p>
