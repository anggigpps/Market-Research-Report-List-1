<p><p>The Dual and Multi-Energy Computed Tomography (CT) market encompasses advanced imaging technologies that enhance diagnostic capabilities. Dual-source CT utilizes two X-ray sources and detectors to capture images simultaneously, improving speed and image quality. Rapid kVp switching, on the other hand, allows the rapid alteration of tube voltage during a single scan, enabling differentiation of materials based on atomic number. Both methods enhance tissue characterization, improve contrast resolution, and facilitate precise diagnostic evaluations in various clinical settings.</p></p>
