<p><p>The manual dental scaler market encompasses several types, each designed for specific dental procedures. Sickle scalers feature a curved blade for removing supragingival plaque. Curettes, with their rounded tips, are used for both supragingival and subgingival scaling. Files are designed for heavy calculus removal, while hoes have a flat blade ideal for specific tooth surfaces. Chisels are employed to break down heavy deposits and smooth tooth surfaces. Each type addresses different dental hygiene needs and techniques, enhancing oral care.</p></p>
