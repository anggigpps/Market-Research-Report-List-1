<p><p>The disposable medical catheter market includes various types designed for specific uses. Intermittent catheters are utilized for bladder drainage in patients with urinary retention, allowing for self-catheterization. Foley catheters are indwelling devices used for continuous urine drainage, often in hospital settings or during surgeries. Urological catheters encompass a broader category, including both intermittent and Foley types, catering to diverse urological needs. These products are vital for managing urinary complications and improving patient comfort and hygiene.</p></p>
