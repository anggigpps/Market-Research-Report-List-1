<p><p>Non-relational databases, also known as NoSQL databases, can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise databases are hosted and managed within an organization's own infrastructure, offering greater control over data and security but requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based databases are hosted on third-party providers' servers, providing scalability, flexibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. This model allows organizations to access the latest technologies and updates without the burden of physical management.</p></p>
