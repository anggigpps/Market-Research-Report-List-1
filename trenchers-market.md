<p><p>Trenchers are essential machinery used for excavation in various industries. The market is primarily divided into two types: Wheel Trenchers and Chain Trenchers. Wheel Trenchers use rotating wheels with sharp blades to efficiently cut through soil and rock, making them suitable for softer terrains and broader trenches. In contrast, Chain Trenchers employ a continuous chain equipped with teeth, allowing them to handle tougher materials and create narrower, deeper trenches, making them ideal for utilities installation and landscaping applications.</p></p>
