<p><strong>The Resistance Furnace Power Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Voltage Regulation</li><li>Indirect Voltage Regulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resistance furnace power transformers are essential in melting applications, offering two primary voltage regulation types: direct and indirect. Direct voltage regulation maintains output voltage by adjusting transformer tap settings directly related to load variations, ensuring stable operation. In contrast, indirect voltage regulation employs control systems that modulate input to the transformer to manage output voltage fluctuations indirectly. Both methods aim to optimize efficiency and performance in resistance heating, catering to various industrial demands and ensuring consistent energy delivery.</p></p>
