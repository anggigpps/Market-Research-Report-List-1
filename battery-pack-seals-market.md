<p><strong>The Battery Pack Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicone Sponge</li><li>Rubber Sponge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The battery pack seals market comprises two primary types: silicone sponge and rubber sponge. Silicone sponge seals are known for their excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and flexibility, making them ideal for various battery applications, especially in high-temperature environments. In contrast, rubber sponge seals offer good cushioning and vibration dampening properties, often used where cost-effectiveness and moderate temperature resistance are essential. Both types ensure protection against dust, moisture, and other environmental factors, enhancing battery longevity and performance.</p></p>
