<p><strong>The Electrical Vacuum Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Vacuum Generators</li><li>Multi-stage Vacuum Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical vacuum generators market is primarily divided into two types: single-stage and multi-stage vacuum generators. Single-stage vacuum generators utilize a single impeller to create a vacuum, making them suitable for applications requiring lower vacuum levels. In contrast, multi-stage vacuum generators employ multiple impellers in series, enabling them to achieve deeper vacuum levels and handle larger volumes of air or gas. These distinctions cater to various industrial applications, impacting efficiency, performance, and energy consumption.</p></p>
