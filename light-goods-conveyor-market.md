<p><p>The Light Goods Conveyor Market comprises several key types, each designed for specific handling needs. Drag chain conveyors utilize chains to transport materials securely, while roller conveyors, either driven or gravity-fed, facilitate easy movement of goods. Plastic type conveyors offer versatility and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for varied applications. Slat type conveyors use rigid slats to convey goods, allowing for stability and flexibility in layout. Each type caters to different operational requirements in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and warehousing.</p></p>
