<p><p>Liquid Chromatography Mass Spectrometry (LCMS) is an analytical technique combining liquid chromatography with mass spectrometry to separate and identify compounds in complex mixtures. The market features various types: Single Quadrupole LC-MS, which offers basic quantitative analysis; Triple Quadrupole LC-MS, known for its sensitivity and ability to perform tandem mass spectrometry for detailed structural information; and Ion Trap LC-MS, which excels in trapping ions for enhanced analysis and is suitable for complex samples. Each type serves specific application needs in pharmaceuticals, environmental studies, and biochemistry.</p></p>
