<p><p>The protein shampoo market is primarily segmented into two types: amino acid-based shampoos and hydrolyzed protein shampoos. Amino acid shampoos utilize essential building blocks of proteins, providing gentle cleansing while enhancing moisture retention and promoting hair health. Hydrolyzed protein shampoos contain proteins broken down into smaller fragments, allowing for better absorption and strengthening of hair strands. Both types cater to diverse hair needs, with amino acid shampoos focusing on nourishment and hydrolyzed protein shampoos emphasizing structural repair and protection.</p></p>
