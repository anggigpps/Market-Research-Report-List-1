<p><strong>The Automatic Sliding Door Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Microwave Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic sliding door sensor market primarily features two types: infrared photoelectric sensors and microwave sensors. Infrared photoelectric sensors detect objects by emitting infrared light and measuring reflections, ensuring seamless door operation while minimizing false triggers. In contrast, microwave sensors emit microwave signals that detect movement in their vicinity, providing greater sensitivity and coverage. Both sensor types enhance user convenience, safety, and energy efficiency in automatic sliding doors, making them essential components in commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
