<p><strong>The ISDN Modem Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Basic Rate Interface</li><li>Primary Rate Interface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ISDN modem market is primarily segmented into two types: Basic Rate Interface (BRI) and Primary Rate Interface (PRI). BRI consists of two 64 Kbps B channels and one 16 Kbps D channel, making it suitable for small businesses and residential users requiring basic digital services. In contrast, PRI provides 23 B channels and one D channel in North America, offering higher capacity for larger enterprises needing robust data and voice transmission. Both cater to different connectivity needs and scalability requirements.</p></p>
