<p><p>The POS cash drawer market consists of several types based on how they are activated and connected. Printer-driven cash drawers open when tied to a receipt printer via a cable, ensuring synchronized operation. Ethernet-driven cash drawers connect through a network, allowing remote control and integration in modern systems. Serial and USB-driven cash drawers utilize serial or USB connections for activation, offering flexibility in setup. Manually-driven cash drawers require physical action to open, typically used in less technologically advanced setups.</p></p>
