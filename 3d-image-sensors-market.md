<p><p>The 3D image sensors market comprises various types, primarily CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide Semiconductor) sensors, along with other technologies. CCD sensors are known for their high-quality images and low noise, making them ideal for professional applications. In contrast, CMOS sensors are favored for their lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them widely used in consumer electronics. Other types may include specialized sensors like time-of-flight or depth mapping technologies, enhancing various imaging applications.</p></p>
