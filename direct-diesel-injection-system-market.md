<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Direct Diesel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System</li><li>Unit Injector Injection System</li><li>Common Rail Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Diesel Injection System market comprises three main types: the Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System, which uses high-pressure pumps linked to individual nozzles for precise fuel delivery; the Unit Injector System, combining the fuel pump and injector in a single unit, offering improved efficiency and space savings; and the Common Rail Injection System, which stores fuel in a rail and allows multiple injections per cycle, enhancing performance and emissions control. Each system caters to specific performance and efficiency needs in diesel engines.</p></p>
