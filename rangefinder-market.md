<p><strong>The Rangefinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Rangefinder</li><li>Acoustic Rangefinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rangefinder market is primarily classified into two types: photoelectric and acoustic rangefinders. Photoelectric rangefinders utilize light waves, often lasers, to measure distances by calculating the time it takes for emitted light to return after reflecting off an object. They are commonly used in surveying and photography. On the other hand, acoustic rangefinders use sound waves to determine distances, making them suitable for underwater applications and topographic mapping. Both technologies cater to distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
