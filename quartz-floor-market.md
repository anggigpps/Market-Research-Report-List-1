<p><strong>The Quartz Floor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Floor Market is primarily divided into two types: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing quartz materials under high pressure to create durable floor panels, ensuring a uniform density and finish. In contrast, casting molding utilizes liquid quartz that is poured into molds and allowed to harden, allowing for intricate designs and shapes. Both methods offer unique advantages, with press molding focusing on strength and durability while casting molding emphasizes versatility and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
