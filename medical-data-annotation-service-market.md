<p><p>The Medical Data Annotation Service market encompasses several key types of annotation techniques. Image Segmentation divides medical images into relevant segments for precise analysis, while Image Classification categorizes entire images based on diagnostic criteria. Polygonal Annotation outlines the shape of specific anatomical structures for detailed study, and Bounding Box Annotation frames areas of interest with rectangular boxes to facilitate object recognition. Together, these techniques enhance the accuracy of machine learning models in medical imaging, supporting improved diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
