<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Life Support Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Venoarterial (VA) ECMO</li><li>Venovenous (VV) ECMO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extracorporeal Life Support (ECLS) devices market is primarily categorized into two types: Venoarterial (VA) ECMO and Venovenous (VV) ECMO. VA ECMO is used for patients with severe cardiac failure, providing cardiac and pulmonary support by draining blood from a vein and returning it to an artery. In contrast, VV ECMO supports patients with severe respiratory failure by oxygenating blood and returning it to the venous system. Both types are crucial for critical care, improving survival rates in life-threatening conditions.</p></p>
