<p><strong>The Rear Axle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drive</li><li>Dead</li><li>Lift</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rear axle market can be categorized into three types: drive, dead, and lift axles. Drive axles transfer power from the engine to the wheels, enabling vehicle movement and torque distribution. Dead axles, primarily used for support, do not transmit power but assist in load-bearing, commonly found in trailers. Lift axles are designed to raise or lower to manage load distribution, improving handling and tire wear, particularly in heavy-duty vehicles. Each type serves distinct functions in vehicle performance and stability.</p></p>
