<p><p>The Marine Electric Motor market is primarily divided into two types: electric inboard motors and electric outboard motors. Electric inboard motors are installed within the boat's hull, offering a compact and efficient propulsion solution, ideal for larger vessels and long-range cruising. In contrast, electric outboard motors are mounted externally, providing versatility and ease of use, making them popular for smaller boats and recreational applications. Both types emphasize environmental sustainability and enhanced performance in marine transportation.</p></p>
