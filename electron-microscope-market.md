<p><strong>The Electron Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Microscopy (SEM)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron microscope market comprises two primary types: Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) and Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM). SEM provides detailed surface topography and composition analysis by scanning a sample with focused electrons, making it ideal for material science and biology. In contrast, TEM allows researchers to visualize internal structures at atomic resolution by transmitting electrons through ultra-thin samples, offering insights into nanoscale materials and biological specimens. Both techniques are crucial for advancing research across various scientific fields.</p></p>
