<p><p>SOC Reporting Services encompass various reports that assess service organizations' internal controls. The SOC 1 Report focuses on financial reporting controls, ideal for users relying on system-related financial information. SOC 2 Reports evaluate controls related to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy, catering to technology and cloud computing sectors. SOC 2+ is similar but includes tailored criteria for specific industries. SOC 3 Reports offer a summarized version of SOC 2 findings for broader public distribution, enhancing transparency without compromising sensitive details.</p></p>
