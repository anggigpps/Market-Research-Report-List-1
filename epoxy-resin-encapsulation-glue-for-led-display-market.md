<p><p>Epoxy resin encapsulation glue for LED displays is designed to protect electronic components from moisture, dust, and mechanical stress. The market is divided into two main types: two-component epoxy encapsulants, which require mixing a resin and a hardener prior to application for enhanced performance and durability, and one-component epoxy encapsulants, which are ready-to-use formulations that cure when exposed to heat or humidity. Both types provide excellent adhesion, thermal stability, and electrical insulation, crucial for the longevity of LED displays.</p></p>
