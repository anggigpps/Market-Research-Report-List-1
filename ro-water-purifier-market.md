<p><ul><li>POUROWaterPurifier</li><li>POEROWaterPurifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO water purifier market comprises two main types: Point of Use Reverse Osmosis (POU RO) water purifiers and Point of Entry Reverse Osmosis (POE RO) water purifiers. POU RO systems are installed at specific water outlets for individual use, ensuring clean drinking water. In contrast, POE RO systems treat water at the entry point of a home or building, providing purified water for all uses, including cooking and bathing. Both types cater to varying consumer needs for water quality and accessibility.</p></p>
