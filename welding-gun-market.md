<p><strong>The Welding Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arc Welding</li><li>Resistance welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding gun market primarily comprises two types: arc welding and resistance welding. Arc welding guns utilize an electrical arc to melt and join materials, offering versatility across various applications and metals. In contrast, resistance welding employs heat generated from electric resistance to fuse workpieces together, making it efficient for high-volume production, particularly in automotive manufacturing. Both methods cater to diverse industries, each with unique advantages that enhance productivity, quality, and precision in metal fabrication processes.</p></p>
