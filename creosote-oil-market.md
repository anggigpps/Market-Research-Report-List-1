<p><p>The creosote oil market is primarily categorized into two types: wood-tar and coal-tar creosote. Wood-tar creosote is derived from the destructive distillation of wood, mainly used as a preservative in the timber industry due to its natural properties. In contrast, coal-tar creosote is obtained from the carbonization of coal and is commonly utilized in the manufacturing of antiseptics and industrial applications. Both types serve distinct functions, contributing to their respective sectors effectively.</p></p>
