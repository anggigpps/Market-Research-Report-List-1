<p><p>The agricultural irrigation equipment market consists of several key types designed to optimize water usage. Sprinkler irrigation uses overhead systems to distribute water evenly across crops, mimicking natural rainfall. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to plant roots through a network of tubes, minimizing evaporation and runoff. Pivot irrigation involves rotating sprinkler systems on wheeled structures, efficiently irrigating large fields in a circular pattern. Additionally, "Others" encompasses innovative and emerging technologies, such as subsurface irrigation and	image-based systems, enhancing water management strategies.</p></p>
