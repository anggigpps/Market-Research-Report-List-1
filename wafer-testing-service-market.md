<p><strong>The Wafer Testing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parametric Test</li><li>Product Sort Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Testing Service market is primarily categorized into two types: Parametric Test and Product Sort Test. Parametric Test focuses on measuring electrical characteristics to ensure chips meet design specifications, assessing factors like voltage, current, and resistance. In contrast, Product Sort Test involves evaluating fully packaged devices or die for functional performance and reliability, ensuring they perform as intended under various conditions. Both testing types are essential for enhancing yield, quality, and performance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
