<p><ul><li>Laser-drum Ceilometer</li><li>Optical Ceilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceilometer market comprises two primary types: laser-drum ceilometers and optical ceilometers. Laser-drum ceilometers utilize laser technology to measure cloud height and atmospheric conditions, offering high accuracy and range in weather observation. In contrast, optical ceilometers employ light scattering techniques to detect cloud bases and visibility, making them suitable for various applications, including aviation and meteorology. Both types serve critical roles in weather monitoring, aviation safety, and climate research, addressing specific needs within the environmental and meteorological sectors.</p></p>
