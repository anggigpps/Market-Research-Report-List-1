<p><p>Pagers, also known as beepers, have evolved through various market types. Beeper pagers send simple numeric alerts, while voice/tone pagers transmit specific tones or voice messages. Numeric pagers display short numerical codes for retrieval, whereas alphanumeric pagers show both numbers and text, allowing for more detailed messages. Finally, two-way pagers enable users to send and receive messages, facilitating real-time communication. Each type serves distinct user needs, ranging from basic alerts to interactive messaging capabilities.</p></p>
