<p><strong>The Benchtop FTIR Spectrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC</li><li>DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Benchtop FTIR spectrometers are categorized into AC (Alternating Current) and DC (Direct Current) markets based on their operational requirements and applications. The AC market typically involves spectrometers that utilize modulated light sources, allowing for effective measurements of transient signals in various samples. In contrast, the DC market focuses on constant light sources, suitable for stable conditions and continuous measurement of material characteristics. Both types cater to industries like pharmaceuticals, materials science, and environmental monitoring, offering flexibility in analysis methodologies.</p></p>
