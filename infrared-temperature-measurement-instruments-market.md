<p><strong>The Infrared Temperature Measurement Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Temperature Measurement Instruments</li><li>Non-Contact Temperature Measurement Instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared temperature measurement instruments market comprises two primary types: contact and non-contact temperature measurement instruments. Contact instruments, such as thermocouples and thermistors, gauge temperature through direct physical interaction with the object, providing precise readings. In contrast, non-contact instruments, like infrared thermometers and pyrometers, measure temperature from a distance by detecting emitted infrared radiation. Both types cater to various industries, offering options based on accuracy, response time, and the nature of the material being measured.</p></p>
