<p><ul><li>Colloidal Gold Type</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mycoplasma Detection Kit market is categorized mainly into two types: Colloidal Gold Type and Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA). The Colloidal Gold Type utilizes gold nanoparticles to detect mycoplasma through a visual change, offering rapid results and simplicity in use. In contrast, the ELISA method employs enzyme-labeled antibodies for more sensitive quantification, typically yielding higher specificity and accuracy in detecting mycoplasma contamination in various samples. Both types are essential for ensuring the quality and safety of biological products.</p></p>
