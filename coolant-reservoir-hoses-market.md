<p><p>Coolant reservoir hoses are vital components in vehicle cooling systems, ensuring efficient circulation of coolant. The market is segmented into upper and lower types. Upper hoses connect the coolant reservoir to the engine, facilitating the flow of coolant into the cooling system. Lower hoses typically link the reservoir to the radiator, allowing excess coolant to return and maintain optimal levels. Both types are essential for preventing overheating and ensuring effective engine performance, with distinct designs to accommodate their specific functions.</p></p>
