<p><p>Swing check valves are mechanical devices designed to allow fluid to flow in one direction while preventing backflow. These valves operate using a disc that swings on a hinge, opening when the pressure of the fluid exceeds the pressure from the opposite side. They are commonly used in various applications such as water supply systems, sewage treatment, and industrial processes.</p><p>The Swing Check Valves Market is experiencing notable growth, driven by increasing demand in sectors like oil and gas, water management, and HVAC systems.
