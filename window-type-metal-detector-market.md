<p><p>The window-type metal detector market comprises two main types: balanced coil systems and ferrous-in-foil systems. Balanced coil systems utilize dual coils to detect metal by measuring variations in electromagnetic fields, providing high sensitivity to various metal types. In contrast, ferrous-in-foil systems are specifically designed to identify ferrous metals within foil packaging, making them ideal for food safety and production environments. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product integrity and safety across various industries, including food processing and manufacturing.</p></p>
