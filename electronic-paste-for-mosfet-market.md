<p><p>Electronic paste for MOSFET applications consists of three main types: medium paste, resistance paste, and conductor paste. Medium paste is primarily used for bonding and thermal management, offering a balance between conductivity and insulation. Resistance paste, designed for high-temperature applications, ensures stable electrical properties while managing heat. Conductor paste, containing metal particles, provides excellent conductivity, essential for efficient electrical performance in devices. Together, these pastes enhance the reliability and efficiency of MOSFETs in various electronic applications.</p></p>
