<p><p>Smart aquaculture systems can be categorized into open and closed systems. Open systems leverage natural water bodies, allowing for the integration of aquatic environments to enhance growth. However, they face challenges like water quality control and disease management. In contrast, closed systems utilize controlled environments, such as recirculating aquaculture systems (RAS), which enable better management of water quality and reduce disease risks. These systems support sustainable practices by minimizing environmental impact and optimizing resource use.</p></p>
