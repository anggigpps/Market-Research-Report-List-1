<p><ul><li>Single Acting Hydraulic Cylinder</li><li>Double Acting Hydraulic Cylinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic cylinder market primarily comprises two types: single acting and double acting cylinders. Single acting hydraulic cylinders use hydraulic pressure to extend the piston but rely on external forces or springs to retract, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, double acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure for both extending and retracting the piston, providing greater control and force throughout the operation. These cylinders are widely used in various industries, including construction, manufacturing, and automotive applications.</p></p>
