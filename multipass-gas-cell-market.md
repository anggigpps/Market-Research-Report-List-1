<p><p>The Multipass Gas Cell market includes various cell types used for gas analysis in spectroscopy. The Pfund cell features a compact design with multiple reflections, enhancing sensitivity. The White cell, utilizing a cylindrical shape, achieves long optical paths for improved measurement accuracy. Herriott cells are known for their rectangular structure and flexibility in alignment, ideal for diverse experiments. Circular multipass cells leverage circular mirrors for efficient light trapping, enhancing interaction time with the gas sample, thus boosting detection limits across various applications.</p></p>
