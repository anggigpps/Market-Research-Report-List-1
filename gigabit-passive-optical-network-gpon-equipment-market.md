<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) equipment market comprises three main components: </p><p>1. **Optical Line Terminal (OLT)**: This central device manages data traffic and connects the service provider's network to the optical fibers, enabling high-speed internet delivery.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Optical Network Terminal (ONT)**: Installed at the customer's premises, ONTs convert optical signals into electrical signals for end-user devices, providing internet connectivity.</p><p>3. **Passive Optical Splitters**: These devices distribute the optical signal from the OLT to multiple ONTs, optimizing network efficiency without requiring electrical power.</p></p>
